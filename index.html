About Me

Having been ill for a long time with anxiety and depression I decided I needed to do something constructive to occupy my time. I first studied for, and graduated with, an honours degree in literature, having had vague ideas about becoming a teacher. After that I discovered the Python programming language and discovered that I love writing code. The opportunity to attend this front end web development bootcamp arose, which I have eagerly taken up. The world wide web is still in its infancy, and I don't want to merely see what happens to it - I really want to be a part of it.
